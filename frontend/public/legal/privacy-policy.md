# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BuzzMap handles account, marketplace, and platform usage information.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Account details such as name, email address, phone number, and role.
- Profile, business, listing, order, notification, and messaging data needed to operate the service.
- Technical and security-related signals such as authentication events, device or session metadata, and usage activity.

## 2. How We Use Information

- To authenticate users and secure accounts.
- To provide customer, business, and admin platform functionality.
- To support reviews, listings, orders, notifications, messaging, and marketplace operations.
- To detect abuse, enforce platform rules, and improve service reliability.

## 3. Business and Public Content

- Some profile and business information may be visible to other users depending on feature context.
- Reviews, POV submissions, and other user-generated content may be displayed inside the platform experience according to feature design and moderation rules.

## 4. Security and Abuse Prevention

- We use account, session, and activity information to protect the platform and respond to suspicious behavior.
- We may review or retain relevant data where necessary for incident handling, moderation, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

## 5. Data Sharing

- BuzzMap does not publish private account data beyond what is needed to operate the platform experience.
- We may share information with service providers or infrastructure partners that help us run the service, subject to operational and legal requirements.

## 6. Data Retention

- We keep information for as long as it is needed to operate the platform, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect service integrity.
- Retention periods may vary depending on account status, transaction history, safety review, and operational needs.

## 7. Your Choices

- You may update account information available through the product.
- Some data may remain in backups, logs, compliance records, or operational history where required.

## 8. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of BuzzMap after updates means you accept the revised version.

## 9. Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact the BuzzMap team through the official support channels available in the platform.
